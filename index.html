Stray Kids The Sound: Release date and tracklist of SKZ Japanese album

Arpita Adhya

Stray Kids are dropping their first Japanese studio album The Sound and the date of SKZ’s upcoming album has been revealed! Take a look at the tracklist of SKZ The Sound and all about the album’s release below.

After SKZ Replay 2022 which was a rundown of the best of B-sides of the K-pop trendsetters released last year, fans will be getting a new Japanese studio album. The tracklist includes songs like Chill Japanese version, Battle Ground, and more.

Release date of Stray Kids The Sound

Stray Kids’ The Sound will be released on February 22, 2023. The Japanese album of SKZ will comprise multiple new songs along with Japanese renditions of their popular Korean tracks from 2022 including Chill, Thunderous and more.

Tracklist of SKZ The Sound

SKZ The Sound consists of eleven total tracks including one instrumental and six of them are brand new. Take a look at the complete tracklist of Stray Kids’ Japanese album here.

  • THE SOUND (Title track)
  • Battle Ground
  • Lost Me
  • DLMLU
  • Novel
  • CASE 143 -Japanese version
  • CHILL -Japanese version
  • Scars
  • Thunderous Japanese version
  • There
  • THE SOUND (Instrumental)
